CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

By nature, the impact of events on the environment can become highly resource-intensive. In some cases, it entails considerable negative environmental consequences for the host community and population. Large scale events, such as conferences and conventions, are potential major sources of greenhouse gas emissions, pollution, and waste. A single large-scale event can lead to excessive energy consumption and various forms of pollution. It is essential that the organizers of events realize the effects created by their activities so they can look for measures to reduce their environmental impact as well as maintaining brand reputation.

Meeting Room expresses serious concern over environmental issue and its impact on our stakeholders. We attempt to minimize negative impact created by our events on surrounding communities as well as reducing our ecological footprint through approaches which emphasize sustainability.

Our company also enjoys the idea of supporting suppliers and vendors at home. Local sourcing offers obvious cost benefits in itself and at the same time help build community ties and increase employment opportunities of surrounding communities. Events can create financial and educational benefits for destinations and local businesses. Meeting Room employs local staff and works closely with local suppliers to help create jobs and support social enterprises.
